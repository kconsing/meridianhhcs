About Us

We understand that home health plays a vital and intimate role in the ever changing healthcare delivery system in today’s culture and for future generations to come. We honor this privilege of serving the community and hold ourselves to the highest standards to deliver value that our patients and healthcare providers can trust.

Meridian Health Care has been recognized and ranked among the TOP 25% Home Health Care agencies in the United States for three consecutive years and considered one of the TOP 500 agencies nationwide for almost a decade. “Winners are ranked by an analysis of publicly available performance measures in quality outcomes, best practice (process measure) implementation, patient experience (Home Health CAHPS®), quality improvement and consistency, and financial performance.” – © National Research Corporation
